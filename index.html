Tic-Tac-Toe

How to play tic-tac-toe:

  1. Be the first player to align three of your marks (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal to win.
  2. Player X always goes first, followed by Player O.
  3. Take turns clicking on a square to place your mark.
  4. Once a square is marked, it cannot be changed.
  5. Align three of your marks (X or O) horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win.
  6. If all squares are filled and no player has three marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
  7. After a win or tie, click the "Restart" button to play again.

Good luck and have fun!
